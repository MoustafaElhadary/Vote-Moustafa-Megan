Why Chose Us?

Being a part of the SGA has allowed us to witness and be a part of the growth and development of this campus. We also serve as senators and leaders of several student groups has helped us gain a better understating of the student body. We have established meaningful relationships that help us confront issues concerning the students. With our past experiences and current relationships, we hope to take full advantage of the opportunities at Behrend and offer it to the students. We would like to help sustain existing clubs and organizations and encourage the growth of new ones. As Behrend grows in size and in number, we want to encourage and include everyone in the decision making process. If we get elected, we will strive to use our experience and dedication along with your opinions to make progress on our campus.
